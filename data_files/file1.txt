1. Microsoft Certified: Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900)
   - Date Issued: 06/25/2021
   - Certificate Number: H872-1809
   - Brief: This certification validates foundational knowledge of cloud services and how they are provided with Microsoft Azure. It covers core Azure services, cloud concepts, securiy, compliance, privacy, and Azure pricing and support. It’s ideal for individuals starting their journey in cloud computing.
   - Description: The AZ-900 certification is designed for individuals who want to gain a foundational understanding of cloud computing and Microsoft Azure’s core services. It introduces key cloud concepts such as infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and software as a service (SaaS), while also covering Azure’s global infrastructure, pricing models, and security features. This certification is ideal for beginners, whether technical or non-technical, who want to learn how cloud technologies work and how Azure can be used to support business operations. It provides a broad overview without requiring hands-on technical skills, making it a great starting point for anyone exploring a career in cloud computing.

   - Date Issued: August 12, 2021
   - Certificate Number: H938-4998

It explores fundamental AI concepts such as machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and conversational AI. The certification also introduces Azure Cognitive Services and Azure Machine Learning, showing how these tools can make AI accessible without deep programming knowledge. AI-900 is ideal for professionals and students who want to explore AI solutions, understand ethical considerations in AI, and gain awareness of how AI is transforming industries.

DP-900 is well-suited for aspiring data professionals, business analysts, or anyone curious about how modern data solutions work in Azure, even without prior technical experience.
